About Tennis

Tennis is a popular sport played on a rectangular court. The objective is to hit the ball over a net and into the opponent's court, with the goal of scoring points by making it difficult for the opponent to return the ball.

The sport can be played in singles or doubles format and is known for its physical and mental challenges.
